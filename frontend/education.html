Food Safety Education

Learn safe food practices and how to prevent foodborne illnesses.

🧼 Wash Hands Properly

Always wash your hands before preparing food and after handling raw meat.

🌡 Cook Food at Safe Temperatures

Ensure meat, poultry, and seafood are cooked thoroughly to kill harmful bacteria.

🥗 Avoid Cross Contamination

Use separate cutting boards and utensils for raw and cooked food.

❄ Store Food Properly

Keep perishable food refrigerated and avoid leaving food outside for long periods.

📅 Check Expiry Dates

Always check food labels and expiry dates before consuming packaged foods.
